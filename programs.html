Our Education Components

Primary Education

Our primary education support ensures children build strong foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. We provide:

  • Full tuition and examination fee coverage
  • School uniforms and essential learning materials
  • After-school homework assistance
  • Extracurricular activities for holistic development
  • Regular progress monitoring and parent-teacher engagement

Secondary Education

We support adolescents through the critical secondary education phase with comprehensive academic and personal development programs:

  • Full coverage of tuition, examination, and practical fees
  • Subject-specific tutoring in sciences, mathematics, and languages
  • Leadership and life skills workshops
  • Career exploration and subject selection guidance
  • STEM enrichment programs for girls

Program Components

Aim and Objectives

The Edu4Talent program has three core objectives that guide all our activities:

  1. Empowerment: Equip youth with market-relevant skills and knowledge
  2. Connection: Bridge the gap between education and employment
  3. Sustainability: Create pathways for long-term career success

Our approach combines practical training with mentorship and real-world experience to prepare youth for the workforce.

Program Components

Nursery & Restoration

Our tree nursery and restoration program focuses on ecosystem rehabilitation:

  • Reforestation projects in degraded areas
  • Environmental education for youth and community members
  • Watershed protection initiatives
  • Biodiversity conservation efforts

Animal Husbandry

Our sustainable livestock program provides nutrition and income opportunities:

  • Poultry projects for eggs and meat production
  • Goat rearing for milk and breeding programs
  • Rabbit keeping as a sustainable protein source
  • Beekeeping for honey production and pollination
  • Veterinary care training for participants

Horticulture

Our horticulture program promotes sustainable food production:

  • Vegetable gardens for nutrition and income
  • Permaculture and organic farming techniques
  • Drip irrigation and water conservation methods
  • Seed saving and propagation training
  • Farmers' market connections for produce sales

Orchard

Our fruit orchard provides long-term food security and environmental benefits:

  • Orchard management skills development
