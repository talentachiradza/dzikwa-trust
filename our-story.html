Our Story

Empowering vulnerable children in Zimbabwe through education and community development since 1995

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 by a group of concerned community members, Dzikwa Trust Fund began as a small initiative to support orphans and vulnerable children in the Dzivarasekwa township of Harare, Zimbabwe.

What started as feeding and clothing 15 children has grown into a comprehensive support system impacting hundreds of children annually through education, nutrition, skills development, and community programs.

The name "Dzikwa" comes from the Shona word meaning "to nurture" - reflecting our commitment to nurturing children to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable children and youth in Zimbabwe through quality education, life skills development, and community support, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and become self-sufficient, responsible citizens.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where every child, regardless of background, has access to quality education and opportunities to realize their full potential, contributing to the development of thriving communities.
