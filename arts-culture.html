Preserving Heritage, Building Identity

Our Arts & Culture program plays a vital role in:

  • Maintaining Zimbabwe's rich cultural traditions for future generations
  • Building self-esteem and cultural pride among participants
  • Providing creative outlets for emotional expression
  • Developing marketable artistic skills
  • Fostering intergenerational knowledge transfer

The program has been recognized by the National Arts Council for its contribution to cultural preservation.
