Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dzikwa Trust Fund

We accept donations through various methods:

  • Online via our secure donation portal on our Donate page
  • Bank transfer to our official accounts (details available upon request)
  • Mobile money transfers (EcoCash, OneMoney)
  • In-person donations at our office in Dzivarasekwa

All donations are tax-deductible and you'll receive a receipt for your records.

Yes! Our child sponsorship program allows you to support a specific child's education and wellbeing. Sponsors receive:

  • Profile information about your sponsored child
  • Regular updates on their progress
  • Opportunity to exchange letters (with appropriate privacy protections)
  • Annual report cards and photos

Visit our sponsorship page to learn more and begin the process.

We welcome volunteers in various capacities:

  • Teaching/Tutoring: Help children with academic subjects
  • Mentoring: Provide guidance and support to youth
  • Skills Training: Share professional skills (ICT, arts, trades)
  • Administrative Support: Assist with office tasks and events
  • Environmental Projects: Participate in reforestation efforts

Both local and international volunteers are welcome. Visit our volunteer page for more information.

We collaborate with businesses, schools, and other organizations through:

  • Corporate Sponsorships: Financial support for specific programs
  • Employee Engagement: Team volunteering or matched giving
  • In-Kind Donations: Provision of goods or services we need
  • Skills-Based Partnerships: Sharing professional expertise
  • Community Initiatives: Joint projects benefiting children

Contact our partnerships team at dzikwatrust@africaonline.co.zw to discuss opportunities.

We welcome visitors to our center in Dzivarasekwa by appointment. To arrange a visit:

  1. Contact us at least one week in advance
  2. Provide details about your group size and interests
  3. Complete any required visitor forms
  4. Review our visitor guidelines to ensure a positive experience for all

Visits typically include a tour of our facilities, meeting staff and children (with appropriate privacy considerations), and learning about our programs. Email visits@dzikwatrust.org to schedule.
