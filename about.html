Our Story of Hope

Dzikwa Trust Fund (Trust Deed M.A. 957/2002) operates in one of Harare's poorest townships Dzivarasekwa with a population of about 250,000. Dzikwa Activity Center at #3147 Rujeko Street, Dzivarasekwa 1, is a beacon of hope for the entire community

Read More

Our Mission

To empower gifted orphans and vulnerable children in Zimbabwe through education & general wellbeing, boosted by digital skills e-learning, performance arts, sports, pyscho-social support & environmental activities

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every child with dignity and care
  • Integrity: We maintain transparency in all our operations
  • Empowerment: We equip children with tools for lifelong success
