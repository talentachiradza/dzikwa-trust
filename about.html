Our Story

Founded in 1995, Dzikwa Trust Fund began as a small initiative to support orphaned and vulnerable children in Zimbabwe. What started as a feeding program for 20 children has grown into a comprehensive community development organization serving hundreds of children annually.

Our name "Dzikwa" comes from the Shona word meaning "to nurture" or "to foster," reflecting our commitment to providing a nurturing environment where children can thrive despite challenging circumstances.

Over the years, we've expanded our programs to address the holistic needs of the children we serve, recognizing that education alone is not enough without proper nutrition, healthcare, and psychosocial support.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable children in Zimbabwe through quality education, nutrition, and community development programs that break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable change.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has access to education, nutrition, and opportunities to realize their full potential and contribute positively to society.
